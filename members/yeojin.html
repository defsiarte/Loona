YeoJin

Her Musical Journey

 Yeojin is the fourth member of Loona. She debuted on January 4, 2017 as a member, and released her solo single album "YeoJin" on January 16, 2017.

 YeoJin is uniquely part of no subunit, stnading as an unit of her own. She has said in interviews that she represents the "/" in Loona 1/3, though she does not perform with them.

 Yeojin is the maknae (youngest member) of Loona, and her nickname is Lentil Bean.

About Her (In Real Life)

  She attended several schools during her childhood, including Seoul Changkyung Elementary School, Daegu Yongkye Elementary School, Dukhwa Middle School, Hansung Girls’ Middle School, Jeonghwa Girls’ Commercial High School, and finally School of Performing Arts Seoul, from which she is expected to graduate in 2021.

 YeoJin was a student at Five Music & Dance Academy in Daegu, South Korea in mid-2016, performing dance covers of I.O.I’s “Pick Me” and Jessie J’s “Bang Bang”.

About Her (In Loonaverse)

 As explained in the description of the 1/3 music videos, YeoJin is lost in the forest, and therefore isn't present in 1/3. While she does appear in Everyday I Love You, most theorists agree that this music video is a dream and the events during the video didn't actually occur, therefore YeoJin isn't actually there.
