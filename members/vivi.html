Vivi

Her Musical Journey

 Vivi is the fifth member of Loona. She debuted on February 14, 2017 as a member, and released her solo single album "ViVi" on April 17, 2017.

 The oldest of three siblings, ViVi was inspired to pursue a career in Kpop after seeing music videos by BIGBANG and 2NE1. Before Loona, she worked primarily as a model.

About Her (In Real Life)

 Starting around 2011, ViVi worked as a freelance model in Hong Kong under the name Viian Wong. She modeled for a variety of projects between 2011–2016, including a video tutorial for Blush Cosmetics, and as a PlayStation model at Asia Game Show 2012. ViVi also participated in a number of short YouTube skits, presumably with friends. ViVi was a student of Hong Kong vocal coach Jony Wong in 2012.

 ViVi most likely became a trainee under Polaris Entertainment/BlockBerry Creative in 2016. Like several other members of LOONA, she was initially a trainee under Polaris Entertainment who transferred over to BlockBerryCreative to participate in the LOONA project.

About Her (In Loonaverse)

 ViVi has naturally blonde hair, but once she was killed and reborn as a cyborg, her hair turned an unnatural color of pink. Her eyes are black, but the left one has the ability to glow, similar to ODD EYE CIRCLE.

 Both ViVi's color, light pink, and her animal, the deer, suggest an innocent character. Light pink is a mixture of white and pink, showing her relation to HeeJin with pink, and white showing her angelic presence. Her animal the deer is also known as a symbol of purity, gentleness, and softness.
