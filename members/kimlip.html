Kim Lip

Her Musical Journey

 Kim Lip is the sixth member of Loona. She debuted on May 15, 2017 as a member, and released her solo single album "Kim Lip" on May 23, 2017.

 Kim Lip was revealed as the sixth member of LOONA on May 15. This came as a surprise to many fans, who expected JinSoul to be the next revealed member after her feature on ViVi’s “Everyday I Need You”. The following August, Kim Lip was revealed as a member of LOONA’s second sub-unit, ODD EYE CIRCLE. She was later confirmed to be the leader of the sub-unit, and described her special talent as being dance.

 Kim Lip met future-LOONA member Chuu in middle school, and they attended all three years of high school together as friends. Her nickname is Lippie.

About Her (In Real Life)

 When Kim Lip was in sixth grade, she joined a dance class after her mother told her she needed to lose weight. This ignited her love of dancing and singing, and she began to dream of becoming an idol. Kim Lip also swam on her school’s swimming team for about seven years.

 Kim Lip auditioned unsuccessfully for multiple agencies before she was contacted through Instagram by Polaris-BlockBerry around January 2016. After being cast by BlockBerry Creative, Kim Lip trained in hopes of being chosen for the LOONA project. She saw other trainees with shorter training periods than her selected first, and she became uncertain in herself. After waiting for one year and three months, Kim Lip was chosen as the sixth member of LOONA.

About Her (In Loonaverse)

 Kim Lip's animal, the owl, represents wisdom and knowledge, which we can also see as her role of leader of ODD EYE CIRCLE. We can also assume that this means she is the leader of the primary colors, and therefore has some control over the other members of OEC. As we see with both JinSoul and Choerry, who go against HeeJin's wish and interact with yyxy, they represent the "dark" side of the mobius strip, which is exactly what their creator, HeeJin, doesn't want.
