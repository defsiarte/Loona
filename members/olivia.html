About Her (In Loonaverse)

 As stated by BlockBerry Creative, Olivia Hye is meant to represent destruction and chaos, which can also be confirmed by her representative color. Unlike the rest of the representative colors, silver and black are on a grayscale, while the rest of the members' colors are on a color wheel.

 As theorized by many, Olivia Hye is a fallen angel, meaning her color was once bright and colorful, but somehow became tainted and was outcasted, similar to Lucifer, a fallen angel in the new testament. And with HeeJin representing the creator of the universe, this would mean they are on two opposite sides of the same coin, like night and day. Many believe HeeJin created Olivia originally with good intentions, but somehow Olivia defied this. Another idea is that HeeJin created Olivia subconsciously, and Olivia represents HeeJin's fears and anxieties surrounding the LOONAVERSE.
